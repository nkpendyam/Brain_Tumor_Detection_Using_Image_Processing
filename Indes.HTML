⚙️ Technology Stack & Rationale

Why EfficientNet-B0?

Acts as a safety gate to reject non-brain inputs (X-rays, photos) with 70% confidence threshold. Chosen for its low latency and high precision, preventing false positives in downstream expensive models.

Why YOLOv11n?

State-of-the-art real-time object detection. Forces the pipeline to focus on local tumor regions rather than global skull anatomy, improving spatial awareness before classification.

Why the 3-Branch Council?

  • SwinV2-Tiny (40%): Windowed attention for global texture mapping.
  • ConvNeXtV2 (30%): Modern CNN for rotation invariance & spatial stability.
  • MONAI (30%): Medical-domain pretraining for clinical feature extraction.
